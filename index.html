Learn more about Smitskis!

  • Our Beginnings

    Dreams Inc. is a Japanese toy company that was founded in 1996 and created many collectibles such as Smitski.

  • Smitskis Are Born

    Smitski's were launched in 2015 by Toru Soeya, the CEO of Dreams Inc. Smitski's were and named after "nook" or" corner" because they were designed to represent little creatures that live in peoples homes.

  • How They Work

    Smitskis are blind box figurines that are glow in the dark. Each series has 6 Smiski variations, plus a secret Smiski that is rare to find! The secret Smiski are distributed randomly and may not always be included in one assort box.

  • Present Day

    Since their debut series called, "Series one" Smitski has grown, launching 18 seires as of 2025. They also created strap keychains, touch lights, hippers, and embroidery stickers.
